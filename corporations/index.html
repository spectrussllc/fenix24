John Anthony Smith

John Anthony Smith has spent over 30 years at the intersection of technology, security, and human-centered business. He founded Conversant Group in July 2009, which evolved into Fenix24 — the World's First Civilian Cybersecurity Force — driven by his conviction that organizations deserve a security partner that treats cybersecurity as a human problem, not just a technical one.

Engaged on projects and consultations with companies both large and small throughout his career, John understands the unique challenges of conducting business across industries — its strategy, quality, and process. He has worked with organizations ranging from regional businesses to global enterprises, bringing the same disciplined, people-first approach to every engagement.

His career began in 1994 at age 14 and he has since founded multiple technology companies, including Computech in 1996 and NetAlliant Technologies in 2002, which he sold in 2008. He has never stopped building, and never stopped learning.
